FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work with startups and small businesses?

Absolutely! We love working with early-stage brands. Our Starter package is designed keeping budget-conscious founders in mind. We can also do custom quotes.

What is included in a "project"?

A project is typically one campaign or deliverable set — e.g. a product shoot, a brand video, or a social content month. We can bundle multiple needs together.

How long does delivery take?

Typical turnaround is 5–10 business days for photography and 10–15 days for video production. Rush delivery is available as an add-on for urgent timelines.

Do you offer long-term retainers?

Yes! Many of our clients are on monthly retainers. Long-term partnerships get priority scheduling, dedicated account managers, and discounted rates.

What formats do you deliver in?

High-res JPG/PNG for photos, MP4/MOV for video. We can also provide platform-optimised versions for Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, and more.

Is travel included for on-location shoots?

Travel within Chennai is included. Outstation shoots require travel + accommodation costs which are billed at actuals — always discussed upfront before any booking.
